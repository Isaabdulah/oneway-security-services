About Oneway Security Services

A locally focused security company built on professionalism, reliability, and respect, serving clients across Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area.

Our Story

Oneway Security Services was founded by Isa, with support from his sister Lejla. Coming from a family with a strong connection to the security industry, we grew up seeing what effective security should look like: strong presence, clear communication, professionalism, and respect for people and property.

That background shaped the way we approach this business today. Instead of treating every site the same, we focus on understanding each property, event, or building properly so we can provide security coverage that is dependable, practical, and well-managed.

What We Stand For

We believe security should help people feel safer and more confident in their environment. That means guards who are:

  • Presentable and professional on every shift
  • Calm and clear when responding to incidents
  • Respectful with residents, customers, guests, and staff
  • Consistent with reporting, accountability, and communication

Whether we are covering a condo, construction site, commercial property, or private event, we aim to provide security that reflects professionalism and earns trust.

Our Values

Built on Real Security Values

Growing up around security work taught us that safety depends on consistency, awareness, and doing the job properly without cutting corners.

Professionalism on Every Shift

Responsive & Local
