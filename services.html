Professional Security Services in Toronto & the GTA

From condos and commercial properties to construction sites and private events, Oneway Security Services provides reliable, licensed, and professional security coverage tailored to your site, risks, and hours of operation.

Core Security Services

Uniformed Security Guards

Professional on-site guards for commercial properties, offices, industrial sites, plazas, residential buildings, and private locations that need a visible and dependable security presence.

Condo Security

Dependable condo and residential security services focused on access monitoring, visitor awareness, building presence, patrols, and resident safety.

Mobile Patrol & Lock-Up

Marked vehicle patrols to check doors, gates, and windows, complete perimeter walks, and provide lock-up or open-up services at scheduled times.

Event Security & Crowd Control

Professional event security for private functions, venues, community gatherings, and special events, including entry monitoring, guest screening, and crowd management.

Construction Site Security

Overnight and weekend construction site coverage to help deter trespassing, vandalism, copper theft, and unauthorized access to active projects.

Alarm Response & Emergency Coverage

Fast-response security coverage for alarm calls, urgent site issues, emergency situations, and short-notice guard deployment when risk levels suddenly increase.

How Our Security Coverage Works

1. Site Assessment

We review your property, schedule, traffic flow, risk level, and site concerns to understand what type of coverage actually fits your needs.

2. Custom Security Plan

You receive a clear coverage plan outlining guard hours, patrol expectations, reporting procedures, and any specific instructions for your property or event.

3. Ongoing Reporting

Our guards provide regular updates and incident reporting so you stay informed about what is happening on-site, even when you are off the property.
